The Network Behind Connect Guernsey

Jasiri Network
Channel Islands Edition
Founding Partner

Jasiri Network

Jasiri Network is a global community dedicated to connecting African diaspora professionals — in the diaspora and on the continent — providing professional development, networking, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Connect Guernsey is proud to be the Channel Islands chapter of Jasiri Network, bringing this global vision to Guernsey's shores and weaving it into the fabric of our island's professional community.

Our members benefit from both a strong local community and connections to a powerful global network of ambitious professionals.

Where It All Began

The Event That Started Everything

Before Connect Guernsey existed on paper, it existed as a spark — ignited at a single evening in Saint Peter Port in August 2025.

16 August 2025
Jasiri Network — Channel Islands Edition
🕐14:00 – 21:00 GMT+1
📍JB Parkers' Bar & Cellar, Saint Peter Port, Guernsey
🌍Hosted by Jasiri Network & Charles Ndiritu

Jasiri Network's first event in the Channel Islands brought together professionals across industries for an evening of purposeful networking and learning. The evening featured a panel discussion with senior leaders across finance, technology, and professional services — exploring leadership, growth, and navigating the global landscape while maintaining strong local roots.

"This evening was the proof of concept for Connect Guernsey. We saw with our own eyes that Guernsey was ready for a professional network like this — the energy in the room was undeniable."

Speakers & Panellists
NM
Nancy Masila
Founder, Jasiri Network

Associate Director, GT
What Came Next

The success of this evening proved that Guernsey's professional community — diverse, ambitious, and deeply connected — deserved a permanent home. Connect Guernsey was founded shortly after to make that vision a reality.
