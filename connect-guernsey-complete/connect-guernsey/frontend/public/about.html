Where it Began

Our Founding Story

Connect Guernsey was founded in November 2025 by a group of professionals who shared a simple belief: that Guernsey deserved a professional network that truly reflected its community — diverse, ambitious, and deeply committed to the island.

What started as a conversation quickly became a movement. Within weeks, connections were being made and support was pouring in from Guernsey's Chamber of Commerce, Innovate Guernsey, and other leading organisations. It became clear that this was something the island had long needed.

We identified a gap — not just in networking opportunities, but in the sense of belonging that professionals from all backgrounds could feel when they built their lives in Guernsey. Connect Guernsey was created to bridge that gap.

We are currently formally incorporating as a company limited by guarantee, with our official launch planned for May 2026. The momentum behind us has been extraordinary, and we are grateful to everyone who has believed in this vision from the start.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build a professional community that brings Guernsey's people closer together, creating connections that drive careers, grow businesses, and strengthen the island.

Our Vision

A Guernsey where every professional has access to a network that supports their growth and helps them contribute to the island's future.

Our Values

Inclusivity, integrity, professional excellence, and a genuine commitment to Guernsey. We believe the strongest communities are built on trust, and we hold ourselves to the highest standard in everything we do.

Our Approach

We connect people first — then let professional value follow naturally. Every event and initiative at Connect Guernsey is designed to create genuine, lasting connections rather than transactional networking.

The Foundation

Our Three Pillars

Everything Connect Guernsey does is anchored to one of these three pillars. Together they form a complete professional ecosystem.

🤝

Networking

Bringing together professionals across Guernsey's industries through social events, topic-specific seminars, and talks designed to forge lasting business relationships.

  • Professional social evenings
  • Industry-specific seminars
  • Guest speaker events
  • Cross-sector mixers
  • Open and members-only formats
🚀

Investment & Entrepreneurship

Collaborating with like-minded organisations to inspire entrepreneurship, foster knowledge growth, and connect members with speakers who shape the business landscape.

  • Collaboration with Innovate Guernsey
  • Investor–founder connections
  • Entrepreneurship workshops
  • Business growth seminars
  • Startup community support
🌱

Career Support & Mentorship

Developing meaningful professional relationships amongst members to accelerate career growth, while mentoring the next generation of Guernsey's talent.

  • Member-to-member mentoring
  • Career development support
  • Young professional outreach
  • Professional skills sessions
  • Industry introductions
